Welcome to Spellbook



In consideration of the looming threat against our Empire, a state of emergency is henceforth declared. All practitioners of the Magical Arts shall contribute new spells to the Grand Archives.

You, along with your team of Spellcasters, have been tasked with the job of resolving kingdom-wide disasters and defeating evil forces, all by collecting spells. To do this, you must travel the board, completing Quests and collecting Runes along the way. Once a valid rune combination is collected, create its associated spell and add it to your Spellbook by scanning it with your mobile device.

Help your team in collecting as many spells as possible before evil strikes. The fate of the realm is in your hands!
